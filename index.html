CARBON SEQUESTRATION

Carbon Sequestration involves the removal and storage of carbon dioxide from the atmosphere. For us at EcoShift Capital Ltd , we leverage on the enormous ability of nature to sequester carbon, particularly forests.Forests store twice as much CO2 as they emit. They are responsible for the sequestration of 25% of global CO2.

MECHANIZED FARMING

After we secure the forests, determine the carbon credit and the revenue generated from it, the next step is the investment of the revenue in Mechanized Farming. We deploy advanced tools and machinery in agricultural processes to improve efficiency, reduce labor, and enhance productivity. It includes equipment such as tractors, harvesters, plows, seed drills, and irrigation systems.

AGRO PROCESSING

EcoShift Capital Ltd will establish eleven agri-industrial processing plants which will create further jobs, meet local consumer demand on certain food stuffs, and generate significant revenues through the export of the processed products

WASTE TO ENERGY

We specialize in converting waste into renewable energy, providing sustainable solutions for waste management and energy generation. The company leverages cutting-edge technologies like anaerobic digestion, gasification, and pyrolysis to transform municipal, industrial, and agricultural waste into electricity, heat, and biofuels.

OUR TECHNOLOGY

LiDAR

Building on our culture of excellence and innovation, we utilize Light Detection and Ranging Technology(LiDAR) , which employs Laser Pulses to scan & surver forests.

The Process generates a dense three-dimensional(3D) geo-referenced point cloud , providing an accurate representation of the forests current canopy cover

dMRV

digital Measurment Reporting & Verification(dMRV) to monitor, evaluate, & validate carbon offset projects

Deployment of tools like satelite imagery, remote sensing, & IoT Sensor to analyze data as transmitted from LiDAR. Thus providing accurate data for the establishment of a baseline measurement of a projects carbon emissions

DLT

We deploy distributed ledger technology(DLT) like blockchain technology to increase the immutable reliability, traceability and transparency of emissions credits

Optimum value of carbon credit are secured and arranged on the Voluntary Carbon Market(VCM) , holding the revenue generated secutrely in blockchain

CLIMATE ADVOCACY & PRESERVATION GUIDLINES

COMMUNITIES

  • Reduce Waste: Implement recycling programs, minimize single-use plastics
  • Sustainable Transportation: Advocate for public transit, carpooling, cycling, and electric vehicles
  • Support Green Spaces: Plant trees, create community gardens, and maintain parks to absorb CO2 and promote biodiversity.
  • Educate and Engage: Host workshops, campaigns, and activities to raise awareness about climate change and sustainable living practices.

INDIVIDUALS

  • Switch to Renewable Energy: Power your home with renewable energy options like solar or wind, or choose green energy plans from your utility provider.
  • Reduce, Reuse, Recycle: Practice mindful consumption, reduce waste, and recycle to minimize landfill emissions.
  • Minimize Single-Use Plastics: Opt for reusable bags, bottles, and containers to cut down on plastic waste.
  • Plant Trees: Participate in local tree-planting initiatives or plant trees in your yard to help absorb CO2.

ORGANIZATIONS

  • Adopt Renewable Energy: Transition to solar, wind, or other renewable energy sources for office buildings, manufacturing, and operations.
  • Offset Carbon Emissions: Invest in carbon offset programs, such as reforestation or renewable energy projects, to neutralize unavoidable emissions.
  • Advocate for Climate Policies: Support and advocate for policies and regulations that promote global climate action and reduce carbon emissions at a governmental level.
