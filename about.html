Company Inception

EcoShift Capital Ltd was established in 2023, to address climate change not as a constraint, but as a powerful driver of economic transformation. Headquartered in the UK, the company was founded on the conviction that sustainable infrastructure, green technology, and renewable energy investments can simultaneously generate environmental impact, resilient economies, and long-term commercial returns. EcoShift Capital integrates climate-conscious strategy with scalable business models to unlock value across emerging markets.

Africa Regional Office Opening

The launch of EcoShift Capital’s Africa regional office in the year 2024, in the city of Abuja represented a strategic milestone in deepening the company’s on-ground presence across the continent. Abuja was selected as a central coordination hub to support project development, regulatory engagement, and regional partnerships. This expansion enhances EcoShift’s ability to execute sustainability-driven investments in renewable energy, mechanized agriculture, agro-processing, and eco-industrial development across multiple African markets.

MEIC - PROJECT ALPHA

The Muchinga Escarpment Forests, located in Zambia, is a flagship sustainability initiative focused on large-scale carbon sequestration and ecosystem preservation within the ecologically significant Muchinga Forest. The Muchinga Eco-Industrial City(MEIC) project combines forest conservation with sustainable land-use practices, community-linked agriculture, and long-term carbon value creation. Through this integrated approach, EcoShift Capital seeks to protect biodiversity, generate verifiable carbon credits, and create durable economic opportunities for local communities.

How does carbon offsetting work?

Carbon offsetting involves compensating for carbon emissions by investing in projects that reduce or remove carbon dioxide from the atmosphere. For example, planting trees or funding renewable energy projects.
